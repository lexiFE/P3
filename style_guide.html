Project 01 - Spread Design

I was choosing the magazine layout design project to At the starting point, I carefully read the requirement of the project which is designated to capture a model composed in an architectural space, and together to unite while representing a theme that would be put on a 3-page spread as in a hypothetical magazine. Then with a primary idea in mind, I started a plan to visit the site and seek around the best scenes and camera angle to prepare the real shooting later on. The shooting procedure went well with given instruction to the model to integrate with the shooting scene elements. Therefore, in post-production; I could have plenty of photos for selection.

By utilizing the digital softwares (Adobe Lightroom 2018, Illustrator 2018) to apply photo treatment on the images into black and white, and adjusted the exposure, sharpness, brightness and many of others, section by section to better optimize the balance of each photo to represent the most out of its theme. I was also inspired by the literal guideline readings and layout collection “Harper’s Bazaar” according to the famous art director Alexey Brodovitch. “Design is an iterative process” always remind me there are lots of diverse methods to play around the possibilities and arrange.

Animation:

Before the animation process began, the team and I would set up a few meetings to propose the settings, character and the plot for each scene, in our self-developed creative animation project. In order to construct a fluent and comprehensive story to the audience, we thought about the doability of shaping our own made characters and the feasibility of the animated functions to the team. Later on, each student is allowed to use the the second individual assignment whose requirement is to realistic, in their later group project’s use. I considered this as an opportunity to start the project ahead and it is beneficial not only to myself but also for the team as well. Therefore, I built the cafe scene in 3 dimensional from scratch.
